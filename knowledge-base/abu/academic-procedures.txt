2. SCHOOL FEES PAYMENT:
Payment Process:
1. Log into ABU Student Portal
2. Generate Transaction ID/Invoice
3. Pay school fees and various charges using Remita
4. Payment can be made online or at designated banks
5. Wait for payment confirmation
6. Print payment receipt from portal

Payment Methods:
- Online payment via Remita
- Bank payment with Transaction ID
- Accepted at designated banks

3. COURSE REGISTRATION:
When to Register:
- After successful payment of school fees
- Within the stipulated registration period
- Typically at the beginning of each semester

How to Register:
1. Log into ABU Student Portal
2. Navigate to Course Registration section
3. Select courses based on curriculum
4. Submit registration
5. Print course registration form
6. Get confirmation from department

Important Notes:
- Must have paid fees to register
- Cannot register without clearing previous semester
- Must meet credit load requirements
- Add/Drop period available (limited time)
- Late registration may attract penalties

EXAMINATIONS:

Types of Assessments:
- Continuous Assessment Tests (CATs)
- Mid-semester examinations (if applicable)
- End-of-semester examinations
- Practical examinations (for applicable courses)
- Some large courses or general studies may use CBT

Examination Rules:
- Must bring student ID to exam hall
- Arrive early
- No electronic devices allowed (unless specified)
- No examination malpractice tolerated
- Dress code must be observed
- Follow all examination regulations

RESULTS:

Result Checking:
- Semester results accessed online
- Log into ABU Student Portal
- View grades and GPA
- Download result slip

Result Processing Timeline:
- Results typically released 4-8 weeks after exams
- Check portal regularly for updates

Grade Point System:
- Letter grades: A, B, C, D, E, F
- Grade points: 5.0 scale
- GPA calculated each semester
- CGPA calculated cumulatively
