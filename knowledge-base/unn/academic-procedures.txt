UNIVERSITY OF NIGERIA, NSUKKA - ACADEMIC PROCEDURES

Student Portal:
UNN utilizes an integrated University Portal for most student applications, registration, and result checking.
Portal URL: https://unnportal.unn.edu.ng/

REGISTRATION PROCESS:

1. Acceptance Fee Payment:
   - Newly admitted students pay a non-refundable acceptance fee
   - Fee: Approximately â‚¦30,000
   - Payment via portal using Remita platform

2. Screening (Fresh Students):
   - Freshers undergo online and/or physical screening
   - Required documents to upload/present:
     * O'Level results (WAEC/NECO)
     * JAMB admission letter
     * Birth certificate/Age declaration
     * Passport photographs
     * Other credentials as specified
   - Verification of credentials

3. School Fees Payment:
   - After acceptance, generate payment invoice (Remita RRR) from portal
   - Fees include:
     * Tuition
     * Accommodation (if allocated hostel)
     * ICT fees
     * Library fees
     * Other university charges
   - Pay online via Remita or at designated banks
   - Keep payment receipt for verification

4. Course Registration:
   - Upon payment confirmation, register for courses online
   - Access portal: https://unnportal.unn.edu.ng/
   - Register within specified period
   - Select courses according to department's curriculum
   - Meet prerequisites for courses

5. Validation:
   - Print course registration forms
   - Submit to Department for validation
   - Course Adviser/HOD validates registration
   - Keep validated copy for records

EXAMINATIONS:

Format:
- Examinations conducted at the end of each semester
- Mix of traditional written exams and Computer-Based Tests (CBTs)
- Continuous assessment during semester
- Final examinations

Requirements:
- Must be properly registered for courses
- Attend minimum required classes (usually 75%)
- Complete all continuous assessments
- Meet all course requirements

RESULTS:

Checking Results:
- Semester results released online
- Login to UNN Portal: https://unnportal.unn.edu.ng/Student/CheckResults.aspx
- Results show course grades, semester GPA, and CGPA
- Grades follow standard university grading system

Official Transcripts:
- Apply through Registry or portal
- Make required payment
- Processing takes time - apply early
- Official stamped transcripts for employment/further studies

Important Portal Links:
- Main Portal: https://unnportal.unn.edu.ng/
- Result Checking: https://unnportal.unn.edu.ng/Student/CheckResults.aspx

Important Notes:
- Keep portal login credentials secure
- Check portal regularly for announcements
- Meet all registration and payment deadlines
- Late registration attracts penalties
- Contact ICT department for technical portal issues
- Different campuses (Nsukka, UNEC, Ituku-Ozalla) may have specific procedures
