FEDERAL UNIVERSITY OF TECHNOLOGY, AKURE - ACADEMIC PROCEDURES

Student Portal:
FUTA utilizes a central Student Portal accessible via the main university website (https://www.futa.edu.ng/) for most academic activities.

REGISTRATION PROCESS:

1. Acceptance Fee Payment:
   - Newly admitted students must pay a non-refundable acceptance fee online via the portal
   - Fee: Approximately ₦20,000 - ₦30,000 (verify current amount on admission guidelines)

2. Screening (Fresh Students):
   - Fresh students undergo screening (often online initially, followed by physical verification)
   - Required documents:
     * O'Level results (WAEC/NECO)
     * JAMB result
     * Birth certificate/Age declaration
     * Passport photographs
     * Other credentials as specified

3. School Fees Payment:
   - After acceptance fee payment and successful screening
   - Log in to the portal
   - Generate payment invoice using Remita
   - Pay school charges online or at designated bank
   - Keep payment receipt for verification

4. Course Registration:
   - Upon payment confirmation, register for courses online through the portal
   - Select courses according to your department's course structure
   - Ensure you meet prerequisites for courses

5. Validation:
   - Print course registration forms
   - Submit to department for validation by Course Adviser/HOD
   - Keep validated copy for records

EXAMINATIONS:

Format:
- Examinations held at the end of each semester
- Mix of written examinations and Computer-Based Tests (CBTs)
- CBTs especially common for large classes and General Studies (GST) courses

Requirements:
- Must be properly registered for the course
- Attend minimum required classes (usually 75%)
- Complete all continuous assessments

RESULTS:

Checking Results:
- Semester results released online
- Log in to student portal to check results
- Results show course grades and semester GPA/CGPA

Important Notes:
- Portal access credentials are critical - keep them secure
- Check portal regularly for announcements and updates
- Contact your department or ICT Centre for technical issues
- Meet all registration and payment deadlines
