FEDERAL UNIVERSITY OF TECHNOLOGY, AKURE - SCHOOL FEES STRUCTURE

Note: These fees are based on the structure announced for the 2023/2024 session. Students should confirm current fees on the portal for the most recent session.

FRESH STUDENTS (Per Session):
Approximately ₦131,000 - ₦150,000+
(Varies slightly based on School/Faculty and specific charges like lab fees)

RETURNING STUDENTS (Per Session):
Approximately ₦80,000 - ₦100,000+
(Varies based on School/Faculty and level)

ACCEPTANCE FEE (Freshers):
Around ₦20,000 - ₦30,000
(Check current admission guidelines for exact amount)

ACCOMMODATION FEE:
Around ₦30,000 - ₦50,000
(If allocated bed space in university hostel)

FEE COMPONENTS:
Specific breakdowns often include charges for:
- Registration
- Library
- ICT/Computer Resources
- Medical/Health Services
- Examination
- Development Levy
- Student Welfare Insurance
- Lab/Studio/Workshop fees (where applicable, especially for Engineering and Sciences)
- Sports and Recreation
- Student Union fees

PAYMENT METHOD:

1. Login to FUTA Student Portal via https://www.futa.edu.ng/
2. Generate payment invoice (Remita Retrieval Reference - RRR)
3. Pay online via Remita platform OR
4. Pay at any bank with your RRR
5. Payment reflects on portal within 24-48 hours
6. Proceed with course registration after payment confirmation

Important Notes:
- All fees are subject to review and may change per session
- Always check the official FUTA portal for current fee schedule
- Late payment may attract penalties
- Keep all payment receipts and evidence
- School fees are paid per session (not per semester)
- Accommodation fees are separate and only paid if allocated hostel space
