NNAMDI AZIKIWE UNIVERSITY - SCHOOL FEES INFORMATION

OVERVIEW:
UNIZIK school fees vary depending on faculty, level, and course type (lab vs. non-lab). Fees were adjusted in recent years.

FEE STRUCTURE:

APPROXIMATE RANGE (PER SESSION):

Fresh Students:
Estimated Range: ₦70,000 - ₦130,000+
- Professional courses typically higher
- Medicine, Law, Engineering, Pharmacy at upper end
- Arts and Humanities typically lower
- Sciences and lab-based courses mid-range

Returning Students:
Estimated Range: ₦50,000 - ₦100,000+
- Slightly lower than freshers
- Same faculty-based variations apply
- Professional courses still higher

ADDITIONAL FEES:

Acceptance Fee:
- Amount: Around ₦40,000 - ₦50,000
- Check current admission letter for exact amount
- Non-refundable
- Paid to secure admission
- One-time payment for new students

Accommodation Fee:
- Range: ₦20,000 - ₦40,000+
- Depends on hostel type and location
- Paid annually
- Subject to availability

PAYMENT STRUCTURE:

Payment Methods:
1. Online payment via Remita
2. Bank payment with RRR
3. Payment at designated banks

Payment Process:
1. Log into UNIZIK Student Portal
2. Generate Remita invoice (RRR)
3. Make payment online or at bank
4. Wait for confirmation
5. Print payment receipt from portal

PAYMENT DEADLINES:
- First Semester: Before resumption
- Second Semester: Before resumption
- Late payment may attract penalties
- Must complete payment before course registration
