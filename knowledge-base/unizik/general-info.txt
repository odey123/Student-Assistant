BRIEF HISTORY:
Nnamdi Azikiwe University (NAU), often called UNIZIK, is a Federal university in Nigeria. Its main campus is located in Awka, Anambra State capital, with a second campus in Nnewi (housing the College of Health Sciences) and a third in Agulu (Faculty of Pharmacy).

The institution started in 1980 as Anambra State University of Technology (ASUTECH). Following the creation of Enugu State and the new Anambra State in 1991, the Awka and Nnewi campuses were constituted into Nnamdi Azikiwe University. In 1992, it was taken over by the Federal Government and named after Dr. Nnamdi Azikiwe, the first President of Nigeria.
