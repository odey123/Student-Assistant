Frequently Asked Questions - Ladoke Akintola University of Technology (LAUTECH)

Q: What programs does LAUTECH offer?
A: LAUTECH offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various fields including Engineering, Pure and Applied Sciences, Agricultural Sciences, Environmental Sciences, and Management Sciences.

Q: How do I apply for admission?
A: Applications are submitted through the Joint Admissions and Matriculation Board (JAMB) for undergraduate programs and directly to the university for postgraduate programs.

Q: What is the minimum JAMB score for admission?
A: The minimum JAMB score varies by program. Please check the university's website or contact admissions for specific program requirements.

Q: Where is LAUTECH located?
A: LAUTECH is located in Ogbomoso, Oyo State, Nigeria.

Q: Is accommodation available on campus?
A: Yes, LAUTECH provides hostel accommodation for students on campus, subject to availability.
