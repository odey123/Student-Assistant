UNIVERSITY OF BENIN - ADMISSION REQUIREMENTS

UTME (JAMB) REQUIREMENTS:

General Cut-off Mark:
- Minimum JAMB score for Post-UTME eligibility: 200
- This is the baseline score to qualify for the Post-UTME screening

Departmental Cut-off Marks:
- Specific departmental cut-offs are determined after Post-UTME screening
- Based on aggregate performance in JAMB and Post-UTME
- Generally higher than 200, especially for competitive courses
- Highly competitive courses (Medicine, Law, Engineering, Pharmacy) typically require much higher scores

O'LEVEL REQUIREMENTS (WAEC/NECO/NABTEB):

General Requirements:
- Minimum of 5 O'Level credit passes in relevant subjects
- Must include English Language and Mathematics
- Credits must be obtained in not more than TWO (2) sittings
- Results from WAEC, NECO, or NABTEB are accepted

Subject Requirements:
- Vary by department
- Science courses require credits in Physics, Chemistry, Biology/Mathematics
- Arts courses require credits in Arts/Social Science subjects
- Always check specific departmental requirements

Special Notes:
- Some courses in College of Medical Sciences may prefer or require ONE sitting
- Medicine, Dentistry, and other medical courses have strict subject requirements
- Engineering and Physical Sciences require Mathematics and Physics

POST-UTME SCREENING:

Requirement:
- All candidates MUST participate in UNIBEN Post-UTME screening
- Registration done online via UNIBEN portal
- Screening fee applies (check portal for current fee)

Admission Criteria:
- Admission based on aggregate of:
  * JAMB score
  * Post-UTME score
  * O'Level results
- Merit list prepared based on aggregate performance

DIRECT ENTRY ADMISSION:

Requirements:
- Acceptable A-Level, NCE, OND, or HND qualifications
- Minimum of Lower Credit for OND/HND
- 5 O'Level credits including English and Mathematics
- Specific subject requirements based on chosen course

Important Notes:
- Admission is highly competitive
- Meeting minimum requirements doesn't guarantee admission
- Cut-off marks vary yearly based on performance
- Always check UNIBEN website and JAMB portal for updates
- Register for Post-UTME immediately after JAMB results are released
