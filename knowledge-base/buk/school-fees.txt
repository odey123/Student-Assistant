BAYERO UNIVERSITY, KANO - SCHOOL FEES INFORMATION

OVERVIEW:
BUK school fees vary based on faculty, level (fresher/returning), and residency status (indigene/non-indigene of Kano State). Fees were increased in recent sessions.

FEE STRUCTURE:

APPROXIMATE RANGE (PER SESSION):

Fresh Students (Non-Indigenes):
Estimated Range: ₦120,000 - ₦220,000+
- Sciences, Medicine, Engineering are often higher
- Arts and Humanities typically lower
- Professional courses at upper end

Fresh Students (Indigenes of Kano State):
Estimated Range: ₦100,000 - ₦200,000+
- Slightly lower than non-indigenes
- Same faculty-based variations apply

Returning Students:
Estimated Range: ₦90,000 - ₦180,000+
- Generally lower than freshers
- Varies by faculty and level

ADDITIONAL FEES:

Acceptance Fee:
- Amount: Around ₦10,000
- Non-refundable
- Paid to secure admission
- One-time payment for new students

Accommodation Fee:
- Range: ₦15,000 - ₦40,000+
- Depends on hostel type
- Paid annually
- Subject to availability

PAYMENT STRUCTURE:

Payment Methods:
1. Online payment via Remita
2. Bank payment with RRR
3. Payment at designated banks

Payment Process:
1. Log into myBUK portal
2. Generate Remita Retrieval Reference (RRR)
3. Make payment online or at bank
4. Wait for confirmation
5. Print payment receipt

PAYMENT DEADLINES:
- First Semester: Before resumption
- Second Semester: Before resumption
- Late payment may attract penalties
- Must complete payment before course registration

INDIGENE VS NON-INDIGENE:
- Indigenes of Kano State pay slightly lower fees
- Non-indigenes pay standard federal rates
- Proof of indigeneship required for lower rates
- Certificate of Origin or Local Government letter needed

FEE VARIATIONS BY FACULTY:

Lower Band:
- Arts and Islamic Studies
- Education
- Some Social Sciences

Mid Band:
- Management Sciences
- Life Sciences
- Physical Sciences
- Communication

Higher Band:
- Engineering
- Computer Science & IT
- Earth & Environmental Sciences
- Pharmaceutical Sciences

Highest Band:
- Medicine and Surgery
- Clinical Sciences

IMPORTANT NOTES:

1. FEE VERIFICATION:
   - Always verify current fees from official sources
   - Fees published on BUK website are authoritative
   - Contact Bursar's Office for clarification

2. PAYMENT EVIDENCE:
   - Keep all payment receipts
   - Save RRR numbers
   - Print portal payment receipts
   - Maintain records for entire program

3. FINANCIAL CLEARANCE:
   - All fees must be paid for registration
   - Clearance required for examinations
   - No outstanding debts for graduation

4. FEE INCREASES:
   - Fees may increase annually
   - Check current rates each session
   - Budget accordingly
