BRIEF HISTORY:
The University of Port Harcourt (UNIPORT) is a federal university located in Port Harcourt (specifically in the Choba/Aluu area), Rivers State, Nigeria. It is a major university in the South-South geopolitical zone.

Established in 1975 as University College, Port Harcourt, it was granted full university status by the Federal Government of Nigeria in 1977. In 1982, the university changed from its original "School" system to a "Faculty" system, which is the structure it maintains today.
