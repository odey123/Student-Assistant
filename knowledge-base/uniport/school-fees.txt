UNIVERSITY OF PORT HARCOURT - SCHOOL FEES INFORMATION

IMPORTANT NOTE:
The figures provided below are estimates based on the 2018/2019 fee schedule, which is the last widely published detailed breakdown. These figures are OUTDATED and subject to SIGNIFICANT INCREASE. Always confirm the current fees on the official ARIS-UNIPORT portal (https://aris.uniport.edu.ng/).

FEE STRUCTURE:

APPROXIMATE RANGE (PER SESSION - BASED ON 2018/2019 DATA):

Programs Include:
- Science
- Engineering
- Education (lab-based)
- Agriculture

Health Sciences:
Estimated Range: ₦97,750 - ₦117,750 (2018 data - CERTAINLY much higher now)
Programs Include:
- Medicine and Surgery
- Nursing
- Dentistry
- Pharmacy
- Other health sciences

Note: The 2018 figures for health sciences seem particularly low and are almost certainly much higher in current sessions.

Returning Students:
Generally lower than freshers' fees
Exact amounts vary by faculty and level
Check ARIS portal for current rates

ADDITIONAL FEES:

Acceptance Fee (Freshers):
- Estimated: ₦50,000 (Based on 2018 data, likely higher now)
- Non-refundable
- Paid to secure admission
- One-time payment for new students

Accommodation Fee (Hostel):
- Estimated Range: ₦20,000 - ₦40,000 (Based on 2018 data, likely higher now)
- Paid annually
- Subject to availability
- Allocation via online system

PAYMENT STRUCTURE:

Payment Methods:
1. Online payment via Remita
2. Bank payment with RRR
3. Payment at designated banks

Payment Process:
1. Log into ARIS-UNIPORT portal
2. Generate payment invoice (Remita RRR)
3. Make payment online or at bank
4. Wait for confirmation
5. Print payment receipt from portal

Payment Options:
- Full payment at once
- Installment payments (if available)
- Check portal for current payment schedule

PAYMENT DEADLINES:
- First Semester: Before resumption
- Second Semester: Before resumption
- Late payment may attract penalties
- Must complete payment before course registration
